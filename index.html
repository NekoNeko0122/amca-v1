What's Inside

Everything you need to master math

AMCA brings together a scientific calculator, camera scanner, quizzes, and structured lessons in one clean app.

Calculator

Scientific calculator for solving expressions and equations across all math domains.

Camera Scan

Point your camera at a math problem and let AMCA scan and process it instantly.

Quizzes

Test your knowledge with subject-specific quizzes. Track progress and improve over time.

Lessons

Structured lessons across Algebra, Geometry, and Statistics to guide your learning journey.

Math Tools

Utility tools for conversions, formula lookups, and step-by-step problem solving.

Three Disciplines
